The Story Behind the Name: The Third Layer

The concept was born one summer evening by the sea, when my 12-year-old son said: "Mom, I feel so happy in my third layer." He explained it like this:

"My first layer is my body, my second layer is my emotions, and my third layer is the part of me that gets happy when the sunlight hits the water."

This insight forms the very foundation of my work. Although we are all shaped by our history and ego, the third layer exists as an underlying source of peace and flow. When we are present there, it feels like love—a space free from demands or judgment.


The Human Condition: Being Present in Every Layer

My workshops are designed to help you live more authentically. As complex beings composed of body, mind, and spirit, it is a fundamental human challenge to remain present in all parts of ourselves simultaneously. Life pulls at us, and we often lose connection to ourselves amidst daily demands and old patterns. However, we can acquire tools to regain that balance. In my workshops, we practice this ability to be present, enabling you to:

✦ Live more authentically: By creating alignment between what you feel, what you think, and who you are.

✦ Achieve greater fulfillment: By learning to navigate from your core rather than your defense mechanisms.

✦ Find meaning: By reconnecting with the source (The Third Layer) where life flows freely.
