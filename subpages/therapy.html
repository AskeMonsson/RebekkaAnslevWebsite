What is Third Layer Therapy?

Therapy

Over many years, I have developed a gentle yet profoundly effective form of therapy. This method was born from a desire to help people become "actively alive" in their bodies, their emotions, and their soulful longings. My approach is a unique blend of the practical and the spiritual; I combine my academic background in coaching and conflict resolution with spiritual training and clairvoyance. This creates a holistic process where our feet are planted firmly on the ground, while our hearts remain open to something greater. The goal is for you to learn how to remain vibrant and present through life's inevitable challenges. Instead of shutting down when things get difficult, I support you in taking ownership of your journey—across all your layers.

Life is an ongoing learning process that requires love, persistence, and patience, balanced with a willingness to act. In these transitions, none of us are meant to do everything alone.

When life hurts—whether we've lost our courage or are trying to push through with sheer will — we all need a helping hand. It isn't always easy to be our authentic self or to hear what our heart is truly longing for.

The body is the vessel for everything we long for; it is through the body that we experience and live life. This is where the truth is felt—both when we are under pressure and when we are at peace. Research has long since confirmed that psychological wounds manifest physically. Therefore, "scars on the soul" can rarely be talked away; they must be felt gently to be released. By involving all three layers—Body, mind, and Spirit—I help you become aware of the patterns holding you back. I support you on the journey from the head to the heart, giving you the courage to move intentionally and act upon your true desires.

Trust is a vulnerable thing, often difficult to maintain throughout a lifetime. We all experience adversity that cracks our trust, teaching us to close our hearts to protect ourselves from future pain. To regain that trust—both in the world and in our own resilience—we must bring awareness to all our layers simultaneously. A central part of this therapy is practicing how to recognize the moments you instinctively shut down, and exploring what is needed for you to dare to open your heart again. I help you find your way back to your core, so you can stand securely in your own light and strength.


We work with everything it means to be human—from practical life experiences and the ego's defense mechanisms to the yearnings of the soul. My primary task is to help you take ownership of your own life.

How a Session Unfolds

A session with me is a safe harbor where there is room for all of you. We can meet in person or online via video call—both formats work equally well for this therapeutic work.

01
The Beginning:

We start with conversation to create an overview. This ensures you feel secure in defining what you wish to gain from our time together. We look at your past and present to gain insight into the patterns you often fall into.

02
The Process:

Once the foundation is laid, we move deeper into the three layers. You don't need to "perform" or achieve anything specific. Everything unfolds at your pace.


Every session is individual. Sometimes we use classic talk therapy throughout the whole session. Other times, it makes sense to work somatically—perhaps on a yoga mat or using light weights. Some clients respond to crystals or chakra work to activate the "life nerve" throughout their system. Whether we work physically or energetically, it is always a collaborative partnership.

Couples Therapy

A Space for Shared Growth

In couples therapy, we create a space for two unique individuals, each bringing their own history, patterns, and longings.

A Space Without Judgment

I meet you as a couple from a place of pure acceptance and love—the Third Layer. My role is to be your guide, holding the space so you can both safely explore your relationship.
In my view, the body is often the most precise compass in a relationship; it tells us when we are closing down in defense and when we dare to open up.

Hearing What is Left Unsaid

I am trained to see underlying intentions and hear what is truly being asked for—even when it isn't clearly stated. I will ask the difficult questions at the right time, so we can look at the triggers and patterns creating distance between you.

Individual Paths to Collective Clarity

Sometimes, individual sessions can complement couples therapy. This allows each of you to become more aware of your own needs and desires before we work on your shared dynamic. This ensures that both partners stand strong in their own light before re-entering the shared space.
